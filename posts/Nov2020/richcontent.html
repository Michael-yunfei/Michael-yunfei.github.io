    Type Mathematics

    Now, let's try to type some mathematic equations. When \(a \ne 0\), there are two solutions to \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\) and they are $$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$$. It looks pretty neat. Now, let's try to do a matrix multiplication $$\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 1 \\ 3 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 2 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 3 & 6 & 9 \\ 4 & 2 & 1 \\ 0& 4 & 7 \end{bmatrix}$$ The derivative \(f'(a)\) of the function \(f(x)\) at the point \(x=a\) is the limit $$f'(a) = \lim_{x \to a} \frac{f(x) - f(a)}{x - a}$$ A more motivating example $$\begin{equation} 1 + \frac{q^2}{(1-q)}+\frac{q^6}{(1-q)(1-q^2)}+\cdots = \prod_{j=0}^{\infty}\frac{1}{(1-q^{5j+2})(1-q^{5j+3})}, \quad\quad \text{for $|q|<1$}. \end{equation}$$
