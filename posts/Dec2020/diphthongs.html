    English Pronunciation: Diphthongs

    • My friend told me that she could tell the difference when I say source and south. Again, I have said so many times that my English pronunciation is terrible. To be honest, I had no idea how source and south should pronounce differently until I learned the difference between vowels and diphthongs.

    Okay, here are the phonemic structures of these two words.

    • source /sɔːs/
    • south /saʊs/
    So, source is pronounced with long vowel /ɔː/, whereas south is pronounced with the diphthong /aʊ/. What's the difference then? The following video is quite helpful.
